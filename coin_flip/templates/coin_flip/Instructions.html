Welcome to the Study!

Welcome! This experiment is about the foundations of human curiosity.

Curiosity - wanting to know more about the world around us - is an inalianable part of who we are as humans. Understanding why we are curuious, and how we choose which questions to ask is an important part of understanding who we are.

In this experiment you will be presented with a series of decisions, and be reimbursed based on your answers. The next pages describe the experiment. After that there will be two practice rounds where you will get a chance to practice, and make sure you understand the instructions. After you confirm that the instructions are clear, you will proceed to paid part of the experiment.
