Welcome to the experiment!

In this experiment you will be presented with a series of decisions, and be reimbursed based on your answers. The next pages describe the experiment. After that there will be three practice rounds where you will get a chance to practice, and make sure you understand the instructions.

After you confirm that you have read and understood the instructions, you will proceed to the part of the experiment. How much you will be paid will depend partly on chance, and partly on the choices that you make in the experiment. You have to complete the experiment until the end to receive any payment.
