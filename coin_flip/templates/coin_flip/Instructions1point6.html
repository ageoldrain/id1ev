Welcome to the Study! Please read the following instructions carefully.

Each round has two questions. First, you will be given the option to find out the outcome of one of the two coins. The first question will ask you which coin (the fair or the biased) you'd like to reveal.

Once you choose the coin, the outcome will be revealed to you on the next page. After that, you will be asked to guess the outcome of both coins.

In each round, each coin is "worth" either $1 or $2. These values are equally likely and will be shown to you at each round. They will, generally, be different in different rounds.

If you guessed correctly in the second question, you will receive the sum of the values of both coins for that round. If not, you will receive $0. You must guess the outcome of both coins correctly to receive the payment. Guessing only one of the two coins correctly is not enough.

You will not see how much you have earned immediately; your earnings will be revealed only at the end of the experiment.

This process will continue for 3 practice rounds and 10 paid rounds. The experiment will end after you complete all 13 rounds.

If you complete the experiment, you will be paid the base payment of $5, plus any earnings you made during the experiment.
