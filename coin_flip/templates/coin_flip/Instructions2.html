Risks

Confidentiality

The following individuals, and or agencies, will be able to view your research records: the investigator(s), research staff, along with other professionals who may be evaluating the research. These includes authorities from Marist University, including the Institutional Review Board ('IRB'), and the United States Office of Human Research Protections ('OHRP').

If at any time you have questions regarding the research, or regarding your participation in the research, you should contact the investigator(s) listed above. If at any time you have questions involving the conduct of this research, or questions about your rights as a research participant, you should contact the Marist University IRB Chair at (845) 575-3000, extension 2692, or email Erik.Moody@Marist.edu

All data resulting from this project will be kept confidential, as will your participation in this project. Data from this project will be used for professional purposes only, and will be stored securely. Confidentiality will be maintained: your name will not be associated with the data we will be obtaining from you. The results of the data collected from this project will be used in academic publications and conferences.

Voluntary Participation

1. Participation in this research study is entirely voluntary. You may refuse to participate or withdraw from participation at any time without jeopardy to your entitlements, although you will forfeit the earning you have made until then. The investigator(s) may withdraw you at their professional discretion.

2. If, during the course of this research project, new information about the aim of our research becomes available, which may relate to your willingness to continue to participate, the investigator(s) will provide this information to you.

3. Any information derived from this research project that personally identifies you will not be voluntarily released or disclosed without your separate consent, except as specifically required by law.

Additional Information

If you have further questions, please contact Dr. Andrew Kosenko at andrew.kosenko@marist.edu.
